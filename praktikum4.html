Rex Regum Qeon or better known as RRQ is an e-sports club from Indonesia that houses 6 game divisions, namely Mobile Legends: Bang Bang , PUBG Mobile , Free Fire , Valorant , and Pok√©mon Unite . Founded in 2013, Rex Regum Qeon has won more than 200 national and international esports tournaments. The name Rex Regum comes from Latin which means 'king of kings' , while Qeon is the in-game name of the President Director of Team RRQ, Riki K. Suliawan. Currently, Rex Regum Qeon has 8 teams from 6 divisions with more than 40 athletes who are not only in Indonesia, but also in Thailand. One of the teams from the PUBG Mobile division , RRQ Oracle, is based in Thailand. Meanwhile, the other 8 teams are based in Jakarta, Indonesia. In 2017, Rex Regum Qeon collaborated with Paris Saint-Germain in forming a Mobile Legends team called PSG RRQ which participated in MPL Indonesia Season 3. In the world of Mobile Legends esports, Rex Regum Qeon has won 4 tournaments, namely MPL Indonesia Season 2 (2018), MPL Indonesia Season 5 (2020), MPL Indonesia Season 6 (2020), and MPL Indonesia Season 9 Rex Regum Qeon has been led by Andrian Pauline as CEO since 2017, when Team RRQ joined MidPlaza Holding. Until now, Rex Regum Qeon has been a 4-time local champion which can be seen on the official Team RRQ jersey or uniform. First, from the Point Blank division, RRQ Endeavor contributed one star after winning the Point Blank International Championship (PBIC) 2017. Second, RRQ Athena, a PUBG Mobile team from Thailand , contributed one star in 2018 from the PUBG Mobile Star Challenge tournament. Third, from RRQ Epic in the Point Blank division , won the Point Blank Word Championship 2019 Russia. Fourth, RRQ Athena together with RRQ Kenboo also won the PUBG Mobile Star Challenge 2019. In 2020, Rex Regum Qeon won various awards for Indonesian esports industry players . Rex Regum Qeon became the Most Favorite Pro Esports Team at the Indonesian Esports Awards 2020. Rex Regum Qeon was also selected as The Most Favorite Esports Team of the Year at the Indonesia Gaming Awards 2020 and as the Esports Team of the Year at Supergamer Fest 2020.
